About Us

WhatsApp DP Optimizer was created to solve the common problem of cropping images when setting WhatsApp profile pictures. Our mission is to provide a simple, free tool that helps users create perfect WhatsApp DPs without losing any part of their images.

Our team of web developers and designers are passionate about creating user-friendly tools that solve real-world problems. We believe in privacy-first solutions, which is why all image processing happens directly in your browser.
